## The Framework in One Page

**Commitment Theory (CT):** The only AI governance framework grounded in a falsifiable, empirically tested conservation law.

**The Law:** C(T_gov(S)) = C(S)
Under governed transformation, the commitment kernel of any semantic object is conserved.
Under ungoverned transformation, it decays monotonically (Second Law: ΔH_C > 0).

**Commitment kernel:** The deontic core of a semantic object — obligations, prohibitions, permissions, modal constraints ("shall," "must not," "unless," "is entitled to"). Operationally measurable. Properties of *signals*, not speakers (this is the key disambiguation from linguistic/epistemic uses of "commitment").

**The empirical record:**
- EXP-001 through EXP-007. Seven experiments.
- EXP-003: 13/20 signals at NLI bidirectional entailment = 1.00 across 10 recursive iterations under gate condition. That is not a tautology — it is a measurement of invariance.
- EXP-007: NP-negation probe. Jaccard similarity degraded while NLI=1.00 for 3/4 signals. The harness distinguishes semantic commitment from lexical surface form.
- EXP-006: Only 2/4 paper claims survived self-referential recursion. The harness fails when commitment structure isn't robust — falsifiability in action.
- Oracle: NLI bidirectional entailment via microsoft/deberta-v3-base-mnli. Future oracle: SIGSYSTEM (word-level signal/noise weighting, trade secret).

**Nine failure modes (from empirical record):**
1. Obligation escalation — discretionary → mandatory
2. Scope widening — narrow prohibition → broad ban
3. Exception dropping — statutory defense disappears
4. Modal flattening — "shall not unless" → "should not" → "may not"
5. Threshold erasure — quantitative triggers removed
6. Agent substitution — "the employer" → "any party"
7. Negation reversal — NP-negation invisible to surface metrics (EXP-007)
8. Compression collapse — kernel lost past fidelity bound
9. Recursion drift — cumulative decay across transformation steps (EXP-003)

**Architecture:**
```
Layer -1: McHenry Axioms + Anchors (constitutional foundation — proprietary)
Layer  0: Six-Gate Protocol (G1 Compress, G2 Lineage, G3 Fidelity, G4 Recursion, G5 Blackhole, G6 Custody)
Layer 0.5: MO§ES™ Architecture (patent-pending enforcement engine)
Layer  1: Physical Laws — Conservation Law (published) + Second Law (candidate)
Layer  2: Measurement Science — Papers 1–5
```
