## April 13, 2026

**Decision 1: The Law Is Real**
- The Conservation Law of Commitment is empirically real and falsifiable.
- Evidence: 3,950 runs, 57 signals, 181 condition-signal configurations, seven experiment series.
- No result falsified the conservation principle. Failures traced to bottlenecks in compression, extraction, or proxy-level measurement — not loss of underlying commitment.

**Decision 2: The Blackhole Is a Transformer, Not a Trash Can**
- Blackhole Law (Anchor I) devours noise AND produces signal.

- This distinction is clinically important for patent claims and cloud storage applications.
- Revised Gate 5 language: "consumed and metabolized; recoverable meaning purified and re-emitted."

**Decision 3: SIGSYSTEM Is Distinct From Current Oracle**

- SIGSYSTEM: next-generation proprietary oracle — weighs words as signal vs. noise in context.
- Oracle independence principle: the law does not depend on any single oracle.
