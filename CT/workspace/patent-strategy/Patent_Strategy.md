## Trade Secret: SIGSYSTEM

**Do NOT patent SIGSYSTEM.** Protect as trade secret instead.

**Why trade secret over patent:**
- Patents require full public disclosure of algorithm; lasts only 20 years
- Trade secret: indefinite protection as long as confidentiality maintained
- SIGSYSTEM's word-weighting algorithm is the "secret sauce" for long-term competitive advantage

**How to establish trade secret:**
1. **Document:** Write down exactly what SIGSYSTEM does and how it weighs words
2. **Access control:** Store code in encrypted, access-controlled repository; limit access strictly
3. **Marking:** Mark all files "CONFIDENTIAL — TRADE SECRET — ELLO CELLO LLC"
4. **NDAs:** Anyone who sees the algorithm signs a binding NDA first
5. **Policy:** Write a formal trade secret protection policy; have all employees acknowledge it
6. **Audits:** Regularly review who has access; revoke immediately when no longer needed
